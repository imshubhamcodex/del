Dark Shadows


"Dark Shadows" is a first-person horror adventure game set in a dark, atmospheric world. Players explore eerie environments filled with supernatural elements, solving puzzles and uncovering hidden mysteries. The game focuses on suspense, where stealth and strategy are key to avoiding terrifying creatures and navigating through haunted settings.


Inspired by gothic horror themes, Dark Shadows immerses players in a world of fear and tension, with its haunting visuals and gripping storyline keeping them on edge throughout the gameplay.

Designed & Built by team DARKX
